Face-Attendance

Facial recognition for attendance tracking (Face Attendance) is a technology that utilizes algorithms to identify and record individuals' presence based on their unique facial characteristics. It works by capturing and analyzing facial features such as face shape, contours, proportions, etc., to accurately and automatically identify a person.
